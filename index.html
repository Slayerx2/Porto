About

I'm Suyash Subedi, a passionate 3D artist and photographer based in Nepal. With a deep love for visual storytelling, I specialize in creating stunning 3D renders using Blender and capturing compelling moments through photography.

My work spans from intricate product visualizations and architectural renders to artistic photography that explores the beauty of everyday life. I believe in the power of visuals to communicate ideas and evoke emotions.

When I'm not behind the camera or modeling in 3D, you can find me exploring new techniques, learning about lighting and composition, or sharing my knowledge with the creative community.
